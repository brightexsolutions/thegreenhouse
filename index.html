Our Story & Vision

A space to
breathe,
worship &
belong.

The Green House is not a performance, not a project, not a programme with lots of logistics and overhead. It's a community of worshippers who share one simple vision: to create a beautiful, low-pressure space in the Lord's presence.

"Mentally, emotionally, spiritually — a space where people especially those in ministry can truly breathe."
  • Open to anyone, from any church or background
  • 40–60 minutes, once a month, Friday evenings
  • Sessions led by different singers each time
  • Rotates across different locations
  • No ticket fees, no dress codes, no pressure

Common Questions

You might be
wondering...

Do I need to be from a specific church to attend? +
Absolutely not. The Green House is open to anyone — from any church, or no church at all. Friends, family, neighbours, colleagues. Everyone is welcome. No gatekeeping, no judgement.
Do I need to be a musician or singer? +
Not at all. Most people attending are simply there to worship and enjoy the Lord's presence. If you're a musician or vocalist and want to be involved in leading, that's a different conversation — reach out via the Join Us page.
Is there a cost to attend? +
Sessions are always free and open. There is no ticket, no offering, no pressure. The Green House is not a revenue-generating venture — it's a community service rooted in love.
How do I join the inner WhatsApp circle? +
The inner circle is for those who feel called to support the vision — vocalists, instrumentalists, or those who simply want to be part of making the sessions happen. Express your interest through the Join Us page and we'll reach out.
How often do sessions happen? +
Once a month, on a Friday evening, from 7–8pm (40 minutes to one hour maximum). The exact date and location will be shared via the community broadcast and on this website.
Can I invite friends who are not believers? +
Yes, absolutely. The Green House is a warm, welcoming space. The sessions are authentic and unforced — a genuine encounter with the Lord's presence that anyone can step into without feeling out of place.

A Typical Evening

What happens at
a session?

🚪
01

Arrive & Settle

Come as you are. Find your spot. There's no dress code, no order of service to follow, no pressure to perform. Just show up and breathe.

🎵
02

Worship Together

Songs, hymns, and moments of spontaneous worship led by a rotating set of voices. Real, unpolished, and beautifully human.

🙏
03

Pray & Reflect

Space to pray together, share what's on your heart, reflect on the week. Not a sermon, not a study — just real talk with God and each other.

💬
04

Share & Connect

Laugh, encourage each other, share testimonies or thoughts. The informal moments between songs are often where the most life happens.

🌿
05

Leave Recharged

Sessions are 40–60 minutes max, intentionally. Short enough to not feel heavy, but meaningful enough to leave you genuinely refreshed.

📅
06

See You Next Month

Once a month. Every month. A rhythm you can actually keep. Stay connected via the community broadcast for dates and locations.
