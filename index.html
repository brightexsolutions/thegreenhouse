Our Story & Vision

A space to breathe,
worship & belong.

The Green House is not a performance, not a project. It's a community of worshippers who share one simple vision: to create a beautiful, low-pressure space in the Lord's presence — four times a year.

"Mentally, emotionally, spiritually — a space where people especially those in ministry can truly breathe."
  • Open to anyone, from any church or background
  • 40–60 minutes, quarterly, Friday evenings
  • Sessions led by different singers each time
  • Rotates across different locations in Nairobi
  • No ticket fees, no dress codes, no pressure

Common Questions

You might be
wondering...

Do I need to be from a specific church? +
Absolutely not. Open to anyone from any church, or no church at all. Everyone is welcome with no strings attached.
Why only quarterly? +
Quarterly keeps it sustainable and meaningful. Four times a year means everyone can plan around it without heavy commitment. Each session feels like a real occasion.
Is there a cost to attend? +
Sessions are always free and open. No ticket, no offering, no pressure. If you feel led to support, visit the Support Us page.
Where are sessions held? +
Sessions rotate across accessible venues in Nairobi. The first session is at Chrisco Donholm Ground on 27 March 2026. Exact locations are shared with registered attendees before each session.
