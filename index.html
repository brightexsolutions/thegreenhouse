Our Story & Vision

A space to breathe,
worship & belong.

The Green House is not a performance, not a project, not a programme with lots of logistics. It's a community of worshippers who share one simple vision: to create a beautiful, low-pressure space in the Lord's presence.

"Mentally, emotionally, spiritually — a space where people especially those in ministry can truly breathe."
  • Open to anyone, from any church or background
  • 40–60 minutes, once a month, Friday evenings
  • Sessions led by different singers each time
  • Rotates across different locations
  • No ticket fees, no dress codes, no pressure

Common Questions

You might be
wondering...

Do I need to be from a specific church to attend? +
Absolutely not. The Green House is open to anyone — from any church, or no church at all. Friends, family, neighbours, colleagues. Everyone is welcome.
Do I need to be a musician or singer? +
Not at all. Most people attending are simply there to worship and enjoy the Lord's presence. If you're a musician or vocalist and want to be involved in leading, reach out via the Join Us page.
Is there a cost to attend? +
Sessions are always free and open. No ticket, no offering, no pressure. The Green House is a community service rooted in love.
How do I join the inner WhatsApp circle? +
The inner circle is for those who feel called to support the vision — vocalists, instrumentalists, or those who want to be part of making the sessions happen. Express your interest through the Join Us page.
How often do sessions happen? +
Once a month, on a Friday evening, from 7–8pm (40 minutes to one hour maximum). The exact date and location will be shared via the community broadcast and on this website.
Can I invite friends who are not believers? +
Yes, absolutely. The Green House is a warm, welcoming space. Sessions are authentic and unforced — a genuine encounter that anyone can step into without feeling out of place.

A Typical Evening

What happens at
a session?

🚪
01

Arrive & Settle

Come as you are. Find your spot. No dress code, no order of service, no pressure. Just show up and breathe.

🎵
02

Worship Together

Songs, hymns, and moments of spontaneous worship led by a rotating set of voices. Real, unpolished, beautifully human.

🙏
03

Pray & Reflect

Space to pray together, share what's on your heart. Not a sermon — just real talk with God and each other.

💬
04

Share & Connect

Laugh, encourage, share testimonies or thoughts. The informal moments between songs are often the most life-giving.

🌿
05

Leave Recharged

Sessions are 40–60 minutes max, intentionally. Short enough not to feel heavy, but meaningful enough to leave you refreshed.

📅
06

See You Next Month

Once a month. Every month. A rhythm you can actually keep. Stay connected for dates and locations.
